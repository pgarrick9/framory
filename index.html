How Well Do You Know Sports History?
📺
The Moment
Each day a new iconic sports moment is revealed through a series of 5 photos — starting with the least revealing. The more photos you need, the lower your score.
🏅
Make Your Guess
Identify who was there and when and where it happened. For individual sports, type the last name of each athlete. For team sports, type the full team name including the city — for example, "New England Patriots" or "Los Angeles Lakers."
⏱
Timing Is Everything
Lock in your answers whenever you're ready — but the earlier you commit, the higher your multiplier. Waiting costs you points.
Scoring tip: Your multiplier starts at 1.0x and drops with every new photo revealed. A perfect guess on Photo 1 is worth more than twice the same guess on Photo 5.
